Implications

Where do we go from here?

Only 1% of women-led startups and 0.7% of ethnic minority founders received VC funding in Europe in 2021. 1 This can make one feel that the state of the European startup ecosystem is a lost cause, but there is power in numbers, and hope when we come together to support the creation of equity for all founders. 2,3

Whether you’re a founder, investor, policymaker, ally, or simply interested in the topic, we’ve collected further resources and tips below to help you in creating a more equitable startup ecosystem. Take a look below.

For Policymakers

Collect basic demographic data on ethnicity and gender 4 to get a clearer picture of the systemic challenges underserved people face. We cannot create change without seeing the problem.

Invest in long-term educational and social equity projects and policies 5

Create a visa that makes it easier for people with migration backgrounds to start a company in Germany. Every fifth startup in Germany is founded by a person with a migration background; however, they frequently face obstacles due to their migration status. A successful example of this is the French Tech Visa for startup employees, founders, and investors 6
